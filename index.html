Frequently asked questions

How long does the service take? +

Most cars take about an hour for a full pair. A single headlight is quicker — usually 30–45 minutes.

How long do the results last? +

With our UV ceramic seal, clarity typically lasts a long time — far longer than a basic polish. Exact longevity depends on sun exposure and care.

Do you really come to me? +

Yes — we're fully mobile. We come to your home or workplace anywhere in our service area at no extra travel charge.

How do I pay? +

We accept all major cards, plus cash and common digital payments. Payment is collected once the work is done and you're happy.

What if it rains or the weather's bad? +

We just need a dry spot — a garage or covered area works great. If the weather doesn't cooperate, we'll reschedule at no charge.

Will restoration really help my night driving? +

Absolutely. Foggy, yellowed lenses scatter and block light. Clearing them lets your bulbs project properly again, so you see more of the road at night.
